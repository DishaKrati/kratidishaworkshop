About Celebrations

Celebrations is an Indian-based full service catering and special events company. Bringing together decades of onsite and off premise catering, we provide a wide variety of menu options and custom services. The Celebrations team is committed to providing a unique guest experience with an emphasis on hospitality and cuisine. Whether you are planning an intimate dinner or arranging an extravagant wedding reception, Celebrations will help ensure the flawless execution of your next event.
